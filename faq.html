Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about bookings, events, payments, and more.

Booking & Payment

How do I book tickets?

Booking is simple:

  1. Go to the event page and click "Book Now"
  2. Choose between tickets or VIP table
  3. Complete M-Pesa payment using the provided Lipa Number
  4. Submit your details with the M-Pesa Transaction ID
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email with your QR code

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept M-Pesa payments only. Use the Lipa na M-Pesa option and enter the business number provided during booking.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Refunds are available up to 48 hours before the event. Contact us via WhatsApp with your booking ID to request a refund. Refunds may take 3-5 business days to process.

When will I receive my QR code?

After your booking is verified (usually within 24 hours), you'll receive a confirmation email with your QR code. Make sure to provide a valid email address during booking. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact us via WhatsApp.

How do I check my booking status?

You can check your booking status on the My Bookings page using your email address. You'll also receive email updates when your booking is confirmed.

Event Day

What time should I arrive?

Doors typically open at 8:00 PM. We recommend arriving by 8:30 PM to secure your spot and enjoy the full experience. VIP table holders can arrive anytime after doors open.

What should I bring to the event?

Essential items:

  • QR Code: Present on your phone or printed
  • Valid ID: Required for entry (national ID, passport, or driver's license)
  • Good vibes: Come ready to have a great time!

Dress code is smart casual. No sportswear or flip-flops.

How does entry work?

At the door, present your QR code (on your phone or printed). Our staff will scan it to verify your booking. Make sure your phone brightness is turned up if showing on screen. After verification, you'll receive a wristband for entry.

Is there parking available?

Limited parking is available at the venue. We strongly recommend using ride-sharing services (Uber, Bolt) or public transport for convenience. See our Venue Information page for detailed directions and parking options.

Is there an age restriction?

All BYZ events are strictly 18+. Valid ID is required at the door for entry. We reserve the right to refuse entry to anyone who cannot provide valid identification.

VIP Tables

What's included in a VIP table package?

VIP table packages include:

  • Reserved table: Your own dedicated space
  • Premium drinks: Cognac VS, mixers, and chasers (varies by tier)
  • Shisha: Included in all packages
  • Group capacity: 10-20 people depending on tier
  • Priority service: Dedicated staff attention

See the booking page for specific details on each tier (Umlando, Yebo, Adiwele).

How many people can fit at a VIP table?

VIP table capacity varies by tier:

  • Umlando (700K): Up to 10 people
  • Yebo (1.2M): Up to 15 people
  • Adiwele (1.8M): Up to 20 people

Can I make special requests for my VIP table?

Yes! Contact us via WhatsApp after booking to discuss special requests like table location preferences, dietary requirements, or celebration arrangements. We'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

How do I know if VIP tables are available?

VIP table availability is shown in real-time on the booking page. Each tier displays how many tables are left. Tables are limited (4 per tier), so book early to secure your spot.

APT Sessions

What is an APT session?

APT (A Private Thing) sessions are exclusive, invite-only events. These are intimate gatherings at private venues with a curated crowd, premium experience, and unique atmosphere. Access is by invitation only.

How do I get invited to an APT session?

Submit an invite request through our APT Request page. We review all requests and will contact you via WhatsApp or Instagram if a slot becomes available. Priority is given to returning BYZ attendees and referrals.

How long does it take to get approved?

Approval timing varies based on availability. We typically review requests within 1-2 weeks. If approved, you'll receive a WhatsApp message with details. If no slots are available, you'll be added to our waitlist and notified when space opens up.

Where are APT sessions held?

APT sessions are held at private, intimate venues in Dar es Salaam. The exact location is shared only with confirmed invitees via WhatsApp or email for security and exclusivity reasons.
