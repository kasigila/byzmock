About BYZ Entertainment

Creating unforgettable experiences through exclusive parties, legendary events, and vibrant community gatherings in Dar es Salaam.

Our Story

Born in Dar es Salaam in 2018, we set out to elevate the city's nightlife—creating spaces where music, community, and culture intersect.

Every Groove night, Tempo event, and APT session is crafted with intention. We're not just throwing parties—we're building a community. Today we've grown into one of Dar's most trusted event brands, partnering with names like Guinness to create nights people remember.
