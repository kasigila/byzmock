About BYZ Entertainment

Creating unforgettable experiences through exclusive parties, legendary events, and vibrant community gatherings in Dar es Salaam.

Our Story

BYZ Entertainment was founded in 2018 with a simple vision: to create unforgettable experiences that bring people together through music, culture, and community. Born in the heart of Dar es Salaam, we saw an opportunity to elevate the city's nightlife scene and give people spaces where they could truly connect.

What started as a passion project has grown into one of Dar's most trusted event brands. We've had the honor of partnering with major brands like Guinness, bringing world-class experiences to our community. But at our core, we're still about one thing: creating nights that people remember.

What makes BYZ unique? We're not just throwing parties—we're building a community. Every Groove night, every Tempo event, every exclusive APT session is crafted with intention. We know our crowd, we know our city, and we know how to create moments that matter.

Our mission is to create spaces where music, community, and culture intersect—where every night becomes a memory worth keeping.
